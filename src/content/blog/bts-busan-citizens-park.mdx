Busan holds a special place in the hearts of ARMY worldwide. As the hometown of Jimin and Jungkook, the coastal city is scattered with childhood memories and iconic photoshoot locations. 

Here is our curated guide to the ultimate BTS pilgrimage in Busan.

<AdSlot />

## 1. Busan Citizens Park (Jimin's Spot)

In 2019, Jimin posted a photo of himself enjoying a quiet walk in Busan Citizens Park. The exact spot has since become a mandatory stop for any ARMY visiting the city. The park is expansive and beautiful, making it a perfect spot for an afternoon stroll.

**Photo Guide:**
Look for the trees near the South Gate. There are even footprints painted on the ground to mark the exact spot where Jimin stood!

## 2. Dadaepo Beach (Jimin's Vlog)

Another one of Jimin's favorite spots. He visited Dadaepo Beach and filmed a vlog here, showcasing the breathtaking sunset. It’s known as one of the best places in Busan to watch the sun go down.

<AdSlot />

## 3. Magnate Cafe

Owned by Jimin's father, Magnate is a spacious, industrial-chic cafe located in the Daeyeon-dong neighborhood. Inside, you can find a mini-museum of fan gifts, stunning art pieces, and even some of Jimin's stage outfits!
