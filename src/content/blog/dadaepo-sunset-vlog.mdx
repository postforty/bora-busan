Dadaepo Beach is famous for its vast sandy shores and spectacular sunset views. It's a place where you can quietly enjoy nature away from the bustling city center.

Many K-Pop stars have visited this beach to capture the golden hour. The reflection of the sky on the wet sand creates a mesmerizing mirror effect, perfect for taking silhouette photos.

## Best Time to Visit

Make sure to arrive at least an hour before sunset to fully enjoy the changing colors of the sky.
