Often called the "Machu Picchu of Busan," Gamcheon Culture Village is a vibrant neighborhood filled with brightly painted houses, quirky art installations, and cozy cafes.

<AdSlot />

## The Little Prince Spot

You can't visit Gamcheon without taking a photo next to the Little Prince statue overlooking the village. It's one of the most iconic spots in Busan.

After exploring the narrow alleyways, relax in one of the hillside cafes and enjoy a panoramic view of the village cascading down to the sea.
